EKON

Whole Slide Scanner is an advanced innovation with latest state of art technology in slide scanning. EKON whole slide imaging provides a novel technology where glass slides are scanned and produces a single digital image. The images are saved in DICOM format along with Patient data. There have been significant advances in whole slide scanning, the hardware and software that have been synchronized for better performance of whole slide scanning. The whole slide images can be viewed in a DICOM viewer, with provisions to annotate, measure and draw polygons. Whole slide imaging has become a reliable diagnosing device to pathologists and scientists for clinical, educational & research purposes.
